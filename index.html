Introducing the MyVIEW 8-week online AFib Life program, tailored specifically for individuals with AFib. 

This program is designed to empower you with the knowledge and tools to manage your condition effectively.

Led by renowned health and wellness experts, you'll receive personalized guidance, educational resources, and evidence-based strategies and information on living life to the fullest with AFib.

During our program, you can expect:

  • Weekly live webinars with expert speakers.

  • Information on the latest advancements in AFib management

  • Nutrition, exercise and sleep management guidance.

  • Mindfulness and stress/anxiety reduction techniques.

  • Symptom monitoring information.

  • Q&A sessions with our health and wellness team.

  • Access to a comprehensive library of educational materials.

  • A supportive community who understands your journey.
